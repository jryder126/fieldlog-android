Training Maxes

Set these once. Targets across the plan are calculated from them. Use ~90% of a real max, or a weight you can do for a solid 5 reps — start conservative.

Units

Pull-up max is the added weight on top of bodyweight. Enter 0 if you train bodyweight only — the app will show "BW" plus any small percentage as added load.

1RM Estimator

Don't know your max? Enter a recent set you took close to failure (≤10 reps is most accurate) and let it estimate your one-rep max — then drop a sensible training max straight into the plan.

Backup & Transfer

Export saves everything — maxes, logs, bodyweight — to one file. Keep it safe. When you get an updated version of this app, open it and import that file to carry your history forward.

Your data lives only on this device, in this app. Clearing your browser data or removing the app can wipe it — export regularly. The export file is versioned, so future versions know how to read it.
